Lunch

My featured lunch reciper is Mac and Cheese!

  • Grilled Cubano Sandwich

    • Marinate pork tenderloin in yellow mustard and pickle juice so the bold, classic flavors of the Cubano sandwich will penetrate the meat. Pressing the sandwich onto the grill with a weight guarantees that the bread gets nice and toasty and the cheese is completely melted.

  • Cobb Salad

    • Creamy avocado is turned into a light, refreshing dressing for a crisp romaine, bacon and citrus-marinated chicken. This colorful dish is best presented as a composed salad, then tossed with the dressing right before serving.

  • Grilled Brunch Tacos

    • Juicy tomatoes and sweet corn are at their peak in the summer, and the grilled salsa in these tacos celebrates both. It is spooned on top of soft scrambled eggs with grilled Spanish chorizo sausage, all wrapped in warm corn tortillas. Be sure to add the cheese while the eggs are fresh out of the pan, so their heat will melt the cheese.

  • Brunch Pizza with Sausage Gravy

    • This recipe delivers all the classic flavors of a Southern breakfast on a pizza! Make the sausage gravy indoors and then turn your outdoor grill into a pizza oven.

  • Greek Panzanella Salad

    • This Greek-inspired take on panzanella, the Italian break salad, celebrates two of summer's best, most abundant vegetables: cucumbers and tomatoes. Grilling the pita, tomatoes and red onions lends a smokiness that goes perfectly with a zesty dressing.

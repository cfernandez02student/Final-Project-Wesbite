    • Stuffing chicken is too much work for a weeknight. Instead, get all the same flavors together in this inside-out version with boneless chicken breasts paired with gruyere cheese and wrapped in ham.

    • Buy diced, peeled butternut squash to cut down on prep time for this pasta that's even heartier when made with whole-wheat or multigrain pasta.

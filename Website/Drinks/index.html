Drinks

My featured drinks recipe is a Bloody Mary!

  • Mojito Limeade

    • Crowd-sized Mojito gets a triple dose lime: It's muddled in at the beginning with mint leaves added to the lime-mint syrup, then poured in with rum and club soda at the end.

  • Cucumber Pineapple Tequila Cooler

    • This smooth summer cocktail made with pineapple and cucumber juices is perfectly balanced and not too sweet.

  • Watermelon Lemonade Slushie

    • This slushie is as beautiful as it is refreshing. Fresh basil is the secret ingrediant: steep it in a simple syrup, then remove it before blending the slushie, so it adds a subtle herbaceous note.

  • Red Sangria

    • The classic sangria, this popular mixed drink is best with a dry red wine to balance out the sweeter oranged and apples.

  • Limoncello

    • Make your own limoncello with just lemon peel and vodka and then let it steep for four days. Add a bit of simple syrup to the mixture then let it sit overnight before straining. You can drink it straight or add it to cocktails.
